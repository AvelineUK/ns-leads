Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Who we are

Somerset Home Services is a lead generation service connecting homeowners in Somerset with local tradespeople. This website is operated by [YOUR NAME / BUSINESS NAME], trading as Somerset Home Services, with a registered address at [REGISTERED ADDRESS].

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at hello@somersethomeservices.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your full name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your postcode
  • A description of the work you need
  • The trade you are requesting

We do not collect any additional personal data, and we do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to connect you with a suitable local tradesperson. Specifically, we:

  • Forward your contact details and job description to a single, vetted local tradesperson who covers your postcode area
  • Send you a confirmation email acknowledging your enquiry
  • Store your enquiry in our secure database for our own records and for billing purposes

We will never sell your data, share it with multiple tradespeople simultaneously, or use it for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interests — specifically, to fulfil the service you have requested by submitting our enquiry form. By submitting the form, you are actively requesting that we share your details with a local tradesperson on your behalf.

Who we share your data with

Your personal data is shared only with the individual tradesperson to whom your enquiry is routed. That tradesperson will use your details solely to respond to your enquiry and carry out the requested work.

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Supabase — secure database storage for enquiry records. Data is stored on servers within the European Economic Area.
  • Resend — email delivery service used to send confirmation and lead emails. Resend processes email content transiently and does not retain it.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

How long we keep your data

We retain enquiry records in our database for up to two years from the date of submission, after which they are permanently deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@somersethomeservices.co.uk.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@somersethomeservices.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have not handled your data correctly.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our database is secured with row-level security policies and is accessible only to authorised personnel.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
