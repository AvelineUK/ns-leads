Finding a reliable painter and decorator can be harder than it looks — especially when national directories send someone with no knowledge of local properties, no care for finish quality, and no accountability once the job is done. We do things differently.

Somerset Home Services works exclusively with painters and decorators who are already based in and around Axbridge and the surrounding villages. They know the local properties, understand the preparation work that older homes often need, and take pride in a finish that lasts.
